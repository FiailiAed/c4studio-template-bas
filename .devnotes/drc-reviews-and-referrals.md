**Two quick implementation notes:**

Add `Txt STOP to opt out` to the first outbound SMS in this flow only, to stay compliant without cluttering every text. And on those auto-responses — vary the *length* slightly too when you post them (don't drop all five back-to-back in one sitting); spacing them out over days is what really keeps them from reading as templated, more than the wording itself.
